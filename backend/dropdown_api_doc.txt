Endpoint: /api/experts/institutions
Method: GET
- Retrieves a list of unique institutions from the database.
Parameters: None


Endpoint: /api/experts/regions
Method: GET
- Retrieves a list of unique regions from the database.
Parameters: None


Endpoint: /api/experts/search
Method: GET
- Searches for experts based on the specified parameters.
Parameters:
field_of_study (optional): The field of study to filter.
institution (optional): The institution to filter.
region (optional): The region to filter.
